About

I don't just take photographs. I change how women see themselves.

I started Valentina Portraiture because I kept watching women apologise for taking up space. Women who were magnificent and had no idea. I built this studio for them.

Every session is private, guided, and completely yours. I work with you before, during, and after — until what you see in those images finally matches what everyone else has always seen in you.

The Experience

Every detail is considered. Every moment is yours.

01
The Consultation

Before anything else, we talk. I want to understand how you feel about yourself right now — and who you want to see in those images. We plan your styling, your vision, and your comfort together. Nothing happens without your say.

02
The Session

Your session is a private, guided experience. Professional hair and makeup. Champagne if you want it. I direct every pose — you never have to wonder what to do with your hands. By the end, you won't want to leave.

03
The Reveal

One week later, you return for your private Image Reveal. You'll see your photographs for the first time together with me. Women cry. They laugh. They say they had no idea. This is always my favourite part.
